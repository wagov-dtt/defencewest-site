## Overview

On Track Recruitment Solutions is a Western Australian-owned labour hire and recruitment business with experience supporting contractors in defence-related and other high-compliance environments. We have supplied skilled personnel to contractors working on RAAF bases in WA, on projects within the Henderson Defence Precinct, and we work directly with Fremantle Ports. This has given us practical exposure to the safety, compliance, security and workforce standards required in controlled project environments. Across civil, rail, mechanical and infrastructure sectors, we focus on supplying reliable, suitably qualified personnel who can meet site-specific requirements and mobilise efficiently. Supported by established recruitment, screening and mobilisation processes, and ISO 9001:2015 certification for Labour Hire and Recruitment, we are well placed to support defence contractors and supply chain partners across Western Australia.
## Capabilities

On Track Recruitment Solutions provides skilled labour hire and recruitment support to defence-related contractors across construction, maintenance, shutdown and project environments. We source hard-to-find workers, pre-screen candidates, verify licences and tickets, and mobilise personnel quickly to meet site requirements. Our capability spans civil, mechanical and rail-related roles, including plant operators, trades, drainers, labourers and track workers. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified for Labour Hire and Recruitment and bring a strong focus on safety, compliance and responsive service. We also work directly with Fremantle Ports, giving us practical awareness of port security and MSIC requirements. Recent defence-related experience includes supplying personnel to contractors working on RAAF bases in WA and on projects within the Henderson Defence Precinct.
