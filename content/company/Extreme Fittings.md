## Overview

Extreme Finishings Pty Ltd – Company History (Defence Industry)

Extreme Finishings Pty Ltd is a Western Australian marine coatings contractor based in Naval Base within the Henderson marine precinct. The company was established to provide specialised marine surface preparation, corrosion remediation, fairing and professional spray application of protective coating systems for commercial, government and defence vessels operating in Western Australia.

With extensive experience working in active shipyard environments, Extreme Finishings supports vessel operators, shipyards and marine contractors requiring high-quality protective coatings and cosmetic finishes. The company has developed a strong reputation for delivering durable marine coating systems that protect critical assets from corrosion while maintaining high presentation standards.

Extreme Finishings has worked on a range of government and defence-related vessels and is recognised as a preferred marine coatings contractor for several Western Australian government vessels operated by the Department of Primary Industries and Regional Development (DPIRD), including Regional Patrol Craft (RPC) vessels. The company has also undertaken coating works on defence vessels such as Coral Snake, providing specialist surface preparation, corrosion repair and spray application services.

Operating within the Henderson marine industrial area allows Extreme Finishings to support local shipyards, contractors and marine operators involved in refit, maintenance and new build projects. The company regularly collaborates with other marine trades and contractors to ensure projects are delivered safely, efficiently and to a high standard.

Extreme Finishings continues to build its capability within the Western Australian marine and defence sector by providing reliable, high-quality marine coating services that extend vessel service life and protect critical maritime assets.
## Capabilities

Extreme Finishings Pty Ltd is a Western Australian marine coatings contractor based in the Henderson marine precinct. The company specialises in surface preparation, corrosion remediation, fairing and the professional application of marine protective coating systems for government, commercial and defence vessels.

Our team supports shipyards, marine contractors and government operators requiring high-quality coating systems that protect critical maritime assets from corrosion and environmental exposure. These services assist in extending vessel service life while maintaining high operational and presentation standards.

Extreme Finishings provides coating services during vessel refits, scheduled maintenance and component repairs within active shipyard environments. Our experience includes work on Regional Patrol Craft (RPC) vessels operated by the Department of Primary Industries and Regional Development (DPIRD), as well as defence-related vessels including Coral Snake.

Our workforce includes personnel with recognised coatings inspection qualifications, including NACE Coating Inspector Level 1 and Level 2 certification, with additional inspection capability being developed through ICorr Coatings Inspector Level 1 training. These qualifications support quality assurance and adherence to recognised protective coatings standards.
