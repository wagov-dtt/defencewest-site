## Overview

Established in 2011, Wittenbaker Engineering Services PTY LTD is Perth based and strategically located in Henderson within close proximity to the Australian Marine Complex. Our knowledge and experience in efficient manufacturing processes, complex mechanical assemblies and design engineering for mechanical components, enables us to provide full circle product and service solutions to highest quality standards exceeding our clients' expectations.
## Capabilities

Wittenbaker Engineering Services PTY LTD with its strategically selected services and high-performance team members aims to be a trusted and reliable partner for the defence sector. Our innovative and solution-oriented approach makes us an effective provider within the Australian Manufacturing industry. Being an established contractor of Civmec, Allship, Luerssen and the Fremantle Foundry we are proud to secure the necessity of an independet high quality manufacturing business supporting the Australian Defence Force needs.
