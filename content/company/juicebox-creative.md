## Overview

Established in 2008, Juicebox is a market-leading digital agency that has delivered design, technology and marketing solutions for some of WA's largest defence organisations and adjacent operators in the space technology, advanced mining and energy sectors, including; ASC, Civmec, Woodside Energy, Curtin University (NRAS), Binar Space and AROSE.
