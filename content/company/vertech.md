## Overview

Our expertise in asset integrity, advanced NDT techniques, and Specialised access solutions within the Oil & Gas industry positions us well to contribute to the maintenance, safety, and performance of defence infrastructure and equipment. We aim to support the defence industry by providing a comprehensive suite of NDT services--including ultrasonic testing, radiographic testing, magnetic particle inspection, eddy current inspection, and more--delivered by certified professionals with deep industry experience.

Vertech recognises that the defence sector requires not only technical excellence but also strict adherence to quality standards, security protocols, and operational discipline. We are actively pursuing the security clearances required to operate within defence environments. Our experience in working under rigorous safety and regulatory frameworks in oil and gas transfers directly to the high standards demanded by defence clients.

We are also exploring partnerships with prime contractors and government agencies to align our services with evolving defence requirements, particularly in Shipyards, and Infrastructure. Our goal is to become a trusted inspection partner, supporting the lifecycle management of critical assets and contributing to national readiness.

With a commitment to innovation, safety, and reliability, The Vertech Group is ready to bring its capabilities to the defence industry and support Australia's defence objectives through world-class inspection and integrity management services.
