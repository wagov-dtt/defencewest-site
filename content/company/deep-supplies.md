## Overview

Reacting to operational challenges observed over 25 years across defence, maritime, and offshore energy sectors, Deep Supplies has developed an intelligent, mission-focused approach to data acquisition and situational awareness.
Backed by a team of highly qualified and experienced operators, our capability is built on delivering reliable, decision-ready information the first time, in both permissive and contested environments. Leveraging advanced sensors, autonomous systems, and integrated software platforms, we provide rapid access to critical data to support operational planning and real-time decision-making.
Our systems are capable of collecting and integrating a wide range of mission-relevant data, including:
Geospatial positioning (GPS-denied and enabled environments)
Bathymetric and subsea terrain mapping
Acoustic and sonar-based detection
Personnel tracking, monitoring, and force protection
Customisable sensor payloads aligned to mission requirements
We have a proven track record supporting both onshore and offshore operations, delivering fit-for-purpose, deployable systems tailored to specific operational needs ensuring capability is delivered right the first time.
Currently, Deep Supplies is advancing AI-enabled systems to enhance autonomous threat detection and operational awareness
## Capabilities

Deep Supplies brings the latest technically advanced system to Australia and New Zealand. These internationally defence approved American and European systems  collect a wide variety of data both below the sea and on the surface. With a range of sensors collecting acoustic data, photogrammetry images, live personnel monitoring and/or a live video feedback to command centres. Our products can save lives and time. Most systems are deployed in under 5 minutes and can have up to 12 months standby times. Our systems can be used for defence situations, as well for detection of invasive marine species, search and rescue, surveillance, maritime support, reconnaissance, special operations, subsea/aerial drug concealments, poaching,  and marine movement.
