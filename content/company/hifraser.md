## Overview

For over 60 years, HIFraser have provided manufacturing and engineering solutions to a number of pivotal national projects. We take pride in the fact we are skilled in managing issues with safety critical items. Employing a combination of expertly trained engineers, technicians and support staff backed up by a robust ISO 9001 management system, our technical knowledge allows us to deliver tailored solutions for each project.
## Capabilities

We represent over 40 world leading original equipment manufacturers. We conduct extensive training, within Australia and at our supplier facilities overseas, allowing us to specify, service and maintain equipment to the highest standard.
We are specialists in:
ENGINEERING CONSULTING: Providing design, documentation, drawings, procurement, project management, investigation and whole life system management. SPECIALIST AREAS: Mechanical, electrical, control & hyperbaric.
MANUFACTURING: Design and CNC machining of components for hydraulic and pneumatic applications. SPECIALIST AREAS: Oxygen compatible components, industrial gas fill systems, short runs, special materials with full certification.
REPAIR & OVERHAUL: Repair, overhaul, installation and recertification at our workshop and onsite. SPECIALIST AREAS: Hale Hamilton valves & systems, marine mechanical systems, hydraulic systems, hyperbaric & hypobaric systems, oxygen & breathing air systems, fire detection & fighting systems, HVAC-R, chilled water units, filtration & separation.
ENVIRONMENTAL SOLUTIONS: Supply, installation and maintenance of waste solutions, vacuum sewage systems and sewage treatment systems. SPECIALIST AREAS: EVAC vacuum toilets, collection & treatment systems, vacuum sewage pipe cleaning, dry waste and wet waste management, facet filtration & separation.
