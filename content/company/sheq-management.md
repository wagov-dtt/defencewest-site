## Overview

Today, SHEQ management in the defence industry is fully integrated, combining risk management, compliance, and continuous improvement to support mission readiness, protect personnel, and meet stringent regulatory and stakeholder expectations across complex, high-risk environments. With over 10 years of application within the sector, SHEQ management systems have matured significantly, evolving into robust, structured frameworks that are embedded into daily operations. These systems are designed not only to ensure compliance with defence and regulatory requirements, but also to proactively identify risks, drive performance, and support informed decision-making. As a result, organisations benefit from consistent processes, strong governance, and a culture of accountability and continuous improvement, all of which are critical to maintaining operational excellence in the defence industry.

## Capabilities

SHEQ Management demonstrates strong capability in supporting the defence industry through the delivery of integrated, practical, and compliance-driven solutions tailored to high-risk and highly regulated environments. With over a decade of experience, we have established robust systems that align with recognised standards such as those developed by the International Organization for Standardization, ensuring consistency, traceability, and continual improvement across all operations. Our capabilities include ISO certification support, covering the development, implementation, and readiness for certification across key standards, as well as Safety Management Systems, internal auditing, incident investigation, and compliance reporting. We also provide specialist support through secondments and onsite advisory services, enabling seamless integration with defence projects and contractors. By combining strong governance frameworks with practical, field-based experience, SHEQ Management supports defence organisations in meeting stringent regulatory requirements, improving workforce safety, and maintaining readiness across complex and dynamic project environments.
