## Overview

DomeShelter Australia is an Australian manufacturer with over 30 years’ experience designing and delivering fit‑for‑purpose fabric shelter solutions to defence and industrial sectors. With more than 9,000 structures delivered in over 50 countries, DomeShelter Australia is recognised for reliable, rapidly deployable infrastructure in demanding environments. 

Supported by in‑house engineering, design and manufacturing teams, DomeShelter Australia delivers consistent, high‑quality solutions aligned to project requirements. We’re proud to be Australian made, owned and operated, focused on supporting operational readiness through practical, durable infrastructure solutions. 
## Capabilities

DomeShelter Australia designs, engineers, manufactures and supplies fabric shelter infrastructure to support defence operations, including rapidly deployable and relocatable systems. 

Key capabilities include: 
* Rapid‑deployment, relocatable shelter systems in configurations tailored to site and operational requirements 
* Configurable structures for hangars, maintenance facilities, warehousing and logistics, training areas, medical facilities and operational bases 
* Solutions engineered for harsh environments, including cyclonic conditions 
* Optional multispectral camouflage to reduce visibility across UV, IR, thermal and radar spectrums, and maintain low signature 
* End‑to‑end project delivery from needs analysis and concept design through to engineering, manufacturing, logistics, installation support and through‑life support, with global deployment capability 
* Ease of integration with third-party systems 
## Discriminators

* Trusted by defence and major industry organisations for over 10 years, including the Australian Government Department of Defence, BAE Systems, Thales and Austal 
* Global footprint with over 9,000 structures, with close to 1,000 of them fully compliant to Wind Region D  
* Australian made, owned and operated, with complete inhouse engineering, design and manufacturing 
* Rapid deployment and relocatable systems that reduce mobilisation time and site disruption 
* Fit-for-purpose solutions engineered to meet specific operational and project requirements
