## Overview

Specialty Metals is a leading distributor of quality titanium, stainless steel and exotic alloys.
As a wholly owned and operated family business in Western Australia, we understand the importance of customer satisfaction and relationships with our clients.
We have an excellent reputation for being a friendly business providing great service, reliability and quality.
Established in Perth in 1987, our diverse customer base spans across market sectors such as defence, energy, mining & minerals processing, chemical processing, fabrication, education, medical, and research & development.
## Capabilities

Our range of premium quality materials include corrosion-resistant, high temperature, chromium-nickel based, nickel-based, cobalt-based, chromium-based and copper-based alloys.
Materials like HAYNES(r) Alloy, HASTELLOY(r) Alloy, ULTIMET(r) Alloy, MONEL(r) Alloy, INCONEL(r) Alloy, INCOLOY(r) Alloy and many more. Along with zirconium, tantalum, niobium, aluminium and carbon steel.
Trading in all kinds of materials and product forms like pipes & fittings, forgings, valves, sheet & plate, heat exchanger tubes and tube sheets.
Drawing from some of the largest producers and stocking distributors across the globe, we specialise in non-standard items and hard-to-find alloys and product forms.
With a focus on quality, the material we provide from our supply chain meets ISO 9001 requirements and incoming material to our store will always undergo positive material identification (PMI) before we accept the goods into our system. The results are electronically filed with the original mill certs for quick retrieval.
