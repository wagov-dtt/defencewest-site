## Overview

First Nations Strategic Alliance (FNSA) is dedicated to equipping Australia's defence force with essential equipment and maintenance services. Harnessing the expertise of FNSA, facilitates timely procurement, dependable maintenance, and logistical support for a wide range of defence assets. Through collaboration and innovation, it acts as a vital link between defence requirements and Indigenous capabilities, fostering economic development and empowerment within Indigenous companies. FNSA serves as a strategic partner, promoting efficiency, sustainability, and national security.

## Capabilities

FNSA provides internation capabilities in shipbuilding and maintenance, military aviation procurement and through-life support, provide engineering and fabrication abilities and provision of spare parts across a broad range of platforms.

## Discriminators

FNSA is Supply Nation Registered, we are a majority Aboriginal Owned company based in Malaga, Perth Western Australia.
