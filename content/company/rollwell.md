## Overview

Rollwell Engineering has completed work for various clients that have dealings with the defence industry.

A major contact involved Rollwell drafting, pressing, welding and fabrication and Surface Treatment of 1000's of Pipe Supports for the OPV1, OPV2 & OPV3 Vessels. We have also had dealing with BAE for a few smaller projects.
