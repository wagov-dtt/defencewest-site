## Overview

Carey has been operating for 26 years in the mining, civil and construction industry including some experience working for Defence as both a head contractor and subcontractor for civil and concrete works. Carey owns and operates the concrete batching plant on Cocos Keeling Islands and has completed several projects on the islands for Defence and other government agencies.
