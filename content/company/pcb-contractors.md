## Overview

PCB Contractors Pty Ltd is a Western Australian–owned and operated construction company
delivering small to medium-scale projects across Defence, infrastructure, commercial and heavy
construction sectors.
Headquartered in Mount Pleasant, PCB Contractors brings together an experienced leadership
team proven to deliver complex projects in live operational environments.
## Capabilities

PCB Contractors delivers construction projects across a range of delivery models including
Design & Construct, Managing Contractor, EPC/EPCM and Construct-Only. Our services and
strengths include:
-Extensive experience delivering Defence base works and live-environment construction
-Commercial and industrial refurbishment
-New building construction
-Demolition and contamination removal
-Infrastructure and external works
-Building services upgrades (electrical, mechanical, hydraulic)
-Fit-outs, alterations and maintenance works
-Strong understanding of Defence HOTO, SDMP and asset data requirements
-Locally based workforce with rapid mobilisation capability
-Flexible delivery models tailored to client risk and program requirements
-Robust safety, quality and document control systems
