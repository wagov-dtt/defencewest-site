## Overview

SMS Group Services is a proudly West Australian-owned company, operating statewide for over 24 years. During this time the business has evolved and grown, now stocking over 3,500 tonnes of scaffolding equipment to suit varying project requirements and employing over 150 multidiscipline personnel.

SMS has kept in touch with the ever-changing dynamics of industry in Western Australia continually customising our offerings to suit our clients' changing requirements. We help identify problems, analyse issues, and work with and listen to our customers to deliver on every requirement. From our recruitment process and selection of our personnel to our rigorous safety management and compliance processes SMS never compromises on quality, safety or value.
## Capabilities

We provide a full range of services including:

- Painting & Blasting Service/ Corrosion Control
- Tailored Scaffolding and Design Services
- Engineered Access Services
- Rope Access Solutions
- Project Management
- Confined Space Management & Atmospheric Monitoring Services
- Shutdown/Maintenance Services
- Provision of Multidiscipline Skilled Personnel
- Scaffolding - Hire or Sale
- Encapsulation and Cladding
