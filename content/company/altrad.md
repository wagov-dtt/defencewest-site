## Overview

Registered in Western Australia in 1985, Altrad Services is a global multi-services provider working in partnership with our clients to build, protect and extend the life of their assets. We deliver complete and innovative solutions, to the highest standards whilst providing a safe working environment for all stakeholders.\
Altrad Services is a leader in the provision of industrial services, generating high added value solutions principally for the Naval Defence, Oil & Gas, Energy, Process and Mining sectors.\
Altrad Services is part of the Altrad Group, who employs around 35,000 people and owns established international brands including Cape, Hertel and Prezioso Linjebygg.
## Capabilities

Altrad Services is uniquely placed to work as a strategic partner through the program life-cycle of Naval Defence fleet.

Altrad Services offers a wide range of cost-driven integrated solutions covering:

- Scaffolding
- Rope Access
- Insulation
- Painting
- Mechanical
- Inspection
- Passive Fire Protection
- Asbestos
- Training & Assessment.

We are here to understand and safely exceed the expectations of our clients. Altrad Services has the ability to provide multi skilled teams to undertake various complex tasks and currently employs more than 1,000 operatives across Australia. Altrad Services has a significant and long-standing pedigree of supporting construction and maintenance activities in the Naval Defence sector. In recent years, we have been involved in prestigious scopes including the Hunter Class Frigates, Collins Class Submarines and Armidale Class Patrol Boats.
