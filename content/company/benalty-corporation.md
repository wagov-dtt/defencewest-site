## Overview

Benalty were approached by Luerssen Australia to build 12 aluminium masts for the SEA1180 OPV vessels for the Australian Navy. 6 have been delivered. we have built aluminium modules for Austal ships for the Cape class vessels built by Austal Ships. Benalty have also built 50 metres of the hull for Austal Ships for the Trinidad Coast Guard. Our 20 metre hovercraft demonstrator has been show cased to several international Defence personnel and also the ADF
