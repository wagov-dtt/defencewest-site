## Overview

Over 35 years experience in high-speed aluminium boat design. Our process creates a ready to go, flat pack kit vessel that can be constructed at any experienced shipyard globally. High Speed Interceptor craft operating in several countries. Our unique Air Rider hull proven military applications and favored for its unique properties under high speed and at rest.
