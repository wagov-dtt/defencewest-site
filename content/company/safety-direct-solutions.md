## Overview

Established in 2004, Safety Direct Solutions Pty Ltd (SDS) is a Registered Training Organisation (RTO #51632) and a recognised leader in medical and emergency response, rescue and industrial safety training, as well as medical and emergency services and personnel across the Australasian region.

Our people are our strength. The SDS team is made up of highly skilled professionals with backgrounds in emergency services, military, healthcare, and specialist rescue, bringing unmatched real-world experience to every project. Whether providing critical medical support, delivering nationally accredited training, or managing emergency response teams on site, our team is prepared, qualified, and committed to excellence.

At SDS, we set the benchmark in safety, preparedness, and response. We are driven by a core belief in professionalism, continuous improvement, and delivering the highest standard of care and training in every environment we operate.
## Capabilities

Training Services
When you need professional training in emergency response, rescue, medical, safety, or fire, SDS delivers with experience and precision. As a Registered Training Organisation (RTO #51632), we provide nationally recognised courses delivered either at our purpose-built training centres or directly on your site.

Medical Services
SDS is a leader in medical response solutions across public, remote, offshore, and industrial locations. Our team provides long-term support and one-off solutions for high or low-risk situations.

Critical Risk Management
The SDS team has invaluable industry experience—our depth of knowledge enables us to deliver critical risk services with precision. From workforce supply to rescue services, we respond to incidents immediately.
