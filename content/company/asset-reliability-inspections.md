## Overview

Operating since 2009, ARI is an Australian Owned NDT and Inspection company with bases in Adelaide, Perth and Bunbury. ARI utilises state of the art equipment and provides subject matter experts to support its operations within manufacturing, sustainment and regulatory compliance. The business has developed innovative and sovereign resources to enable it to quickly scale its operations while maintaining a strict control of quality and consistency.
## Capabilities

Key capabilities include Digital Radiography, Film Radiography, Ultrasonics, Magnetic Particle Inspection, Dye Penetrant Inspection, Phased Array Ultrasonics, Weld Inspection, Welding QC/QA, Pressure Vessel Inspection to AS3788, Pressure Vessel Design as well as Risk Based Inspection (RBI), Non Intrusive Inspection (NII), 3D Photogrammetry and Digital Twins.
## Discriminators

- ISO17025, ISO9001, ISO14001, ISO45001
- Certified Carbon Neutral business
