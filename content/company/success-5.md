## Overview

Success 5 Engineering started in April 2013 with premises in Perth, Western Australia with a vision to provide quality machining services for the local market.

Our highly focused and skilled team strives to meet and exceed our customers expectations so that they can enjoy the convenience of entrusting all their machining needs to us. We provide complementary transport services, unparalleled resources and a flexible working process too.

Our personnel at Success 5 bring an integrated approach to projects as well as extensive engineering manufacturing knowledge and they pride themselves on offering exceptional customer service and commitment to your manufacturing vision. Using your drawings or samples we can manufacture or remanufacture existing components and can offer one-off or batch production to your drawing specifications.

Success 5 have built a reputation based on engineering excellence and an unmatched spectrum of skills, resources and personal project input. This reputation coupled with unbridled enthusiasm ensures that your project will be as important to us as it is to you and will ultimately deliver quality workmanship at a competitive price.
## Capabilities

Success 5 has a 350 sqm, fully equipped workshop in Perth, Western Australia and has the capacity to manage and drive projects of all sizes and complexities through, from inception to completion. We can produce components in a wide range of materials including steels, alloys, plastics, brass, bronze and copper.

With over twenty years experience in design and construction for the commercial and mining industry, we have a team of professionals ready to respond to your design needs and refine your product to produce superior components.

Utilising the latest technology in CNC milling, turning and grinding Success 5 Engineering can take your product to the next level. Our access to the latest technology also ensures a fast and efficient turnaround for your components.

Our facility houses both manual and CNC Multi-axis lathes, CNC milling machines, surface grinders as well as band saws, linisher and drill presses and can provide precision machining to your specifications.

We also have access to machinery to perform multi-axis wire cutting, electric discharge machining, horizontal boring, vertical boring, aluminium and stainless steel welding and fabrication, metal spraying and surface coating. Success 5 aims to be your one-stop-shop for all your fabrication needs and can manage your project through from inception to completion at a very competitive price.

Success 5 specialise in machining components and parts to meet your specific requirements. We can open your drawings created in Solidworks, Autocad and Inventor and in a wide range of file formats. Upon receiving your drawings and specifications we'll work closely with you to ensure that you'll be 100% satisfied with the final product.

Capabilities of our Design and Manufacturing team include, but are not limited to:

- Manual and CNC machining
- Multi- Axis lathing
- Machining Centres
- Horizontal and Vertical Boring
- Wire Erosion / Electric Discharge
- Surface and Cylindrical Grinding

With a commitment to best practice in design and manufacturing our team seek to ensure a high performing product.

Typical components that we have machined include:

- Pins
- Bushes
- Fittings
- Tools
- Adaptors
- Plates
- Cylinders
- Axles
- Wheels

With a flexible approach Success 5 often utilize their manual fabrication skills to ensure that specialized fabrications can be undertaken quickly and efficiently. Through cutting, shaping and joining raw materials manually Success 5 can ensure the highest quality product is achieved in a minimal amount of preparation time.

Success 5 Engineering also have the capacity to carry out maintenance, repair and refurbishment to most components and machines. By efficiently and effectively repairing older machinery before a catastrophic failure we can prolong the life of your machines and limit downtime; saving your business time and money.
