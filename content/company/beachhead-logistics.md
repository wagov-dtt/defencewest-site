## Overview

We incorporated in March 2022, we operated on the Fitzroy river.

## Capabilities

Beachhead Logistics is a company that provides **logistics over the shore and temporary ports** services, which means delivering cargo, materials and equipment directly to and near to the point of use in costal and marine environments. It is the only company in Australia that offers this specialised service. Beachhead Logistics works with the WA Defence Industry, which is a sector that supports the defence of Australia and its national interests. Beachhead Logistics sets itself apart from other organisation by offering a unique capability that can enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of defence operations in challenging terrains.
