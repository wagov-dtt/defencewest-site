## Overview

Land Surveys was established in 1997 and through continued growth and expansion now ranks as one of the best and most reliable surveying companies across Australia and overseas. Through our people, technology and innovation we build long term client partnerships to provide "No Problems, Just Solutions'. We are engaged by clients to provide Surveying, Scanning, Monitoring and UAV Mapping Services, we offer innovative geospatial solutions to meet your project requirements specialising in 3D Reality Capture (tunnel scanning, mobile mapping, image scanning), Digital Engineering services and solutions (GIS Geographic Information Systems, BIM Building Information Modelling and visualisation, Geospatial Data Analytics, Geospatial Data Management), Dilapidation Surveys, Engineering Surveys, Land Surveys and development services , Mining services, Construction Monitoring Services (high rise, tunnels, bulk earth works and volume calculations), Rail services, UAV LiDAR Scanner and Aerial Mapping Services and Mobile Mapping, Utility Underground Detection (clash detection) services and Hydrographic Surveys. Our team consists of more than 300 geospatial professionals across Australia and Papua New Guinea, giving us the capability to scale our team to resource any project. Land Surveys has offices all around Australia in Karratha, Port Hedland, Darwin, Brisbane, Sydney, Melbourne with its head office based in Perth. As a dynamic organisation with long-standing, deep-seated technical expertise Land Surveys can provide a full suite of geospatial services to provide our clients with a multidisciplinary service.
## Capabilities

Land Surveys have worked on all defence bases located with Western Australia, Northern Territory and numerous bases in Queensland, South Australia, Victoria and New South Wales providing surveying and geospatial services to Engineering Consultants and Tier 1 Contractors.

Through the use of cutting-edge surveying technology, we are able to scope and execute surveying projects.
