## Overview

Lucid is an established national engineering consultancy founded in year 2000 that employs 250 professionals in offices located in Adelaide, Alice Springs, Brisbane, Canberra, Darwin, Melbourne, Sydney and Perth. Our vision is to be Australia's most forward thinking, agile and influential engineers and asset advisors.
## Capabilities

Lucid specialises in the provision of advisory, project management and engineering services of projects that are unique, complex and engineering intensive. We provide national coverage with our services offering. Our sector model and Defence Management Team ensures that our offering is consistent on a national basis and meets the expectations of Defence.
