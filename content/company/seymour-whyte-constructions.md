## Overview

Seymour Whyte is a leading Australian infrastructure contractor known for delivering innovative and sustainable construction solutions. As part of the global Vinci Group, we leverage international expertise to execute complex projects across various sectors, including transport, utilities, and defence. Established in 1987, Seymour Whyte has built a strong reputation for quality, safety, and environmental stewardship. Our diverse portfolio includes major road and bridge constructions, water infrastructure, and urban development projects. We specialize in various contract types such as Design and Construct, Early Contractor Involvement, Managing Contractor, and Public-Private Partnerships (PPP). Our team of skilled professionals collaborates with clients, stakeholders, and communities to deliver projects that exceed expectations. We are committed to fostering innovation, embracing new technologies, and driving continuous improvement.
## Capabilities

Seymour Whyte offers a broad range of capabilities specifically tailored to the Defence industry, focusing on areas where we have significant expertise. Our core strengths include **airside and landside infrastructure**, where we provide integrated solutions for Defence airfields, and **civil infrastructure and range development**, supporting military training and operations.

We also specialise in constructing **functional buildings**, ensuring that all structures meet the high operational standards. Our experience in **Guided Weapons and Explosive Ordnance (GWEO)** facilities supports the safe and secure storage and handling of munitions, ensuring compliance with all safety and operational guidelines.

In addition, our expertise in **port and marine structures** allows us to support Defence projects in maritime environments, including the construction of wharves, docks, and other critical infrastructure. We also deliver **smart systems**, integrating advanced technology into Defence infrastructure, and manage **utilities and energy** projects, ensuring reliable energy and utility services to support Defence operations.

These capabilities, combined with our contract expertise in **Head Contract (including D&C), Early Contractor Involvement (ECI)**, **Managing Contractor and** major **Subcontract**, position Seymour Whyte as a versatile and reliable partner for delivering complex Defence infrastructure projects.
## Discriminators

- **R5, B4, F150+ prequalification**: Demonstrates our capacity to deliver large, complex projects, ensuring we meet high standards in safety, quality, and environmental management.
- **Western Australia Open Builders Licence**: Enables us to manage significant construction projects across a broad scope of work, including defence infrastructure.
- **DISP (Defence Industry Security Program)**: Ensures we meet stringent security standards for handling sensitive defence projects, aligning with national security protocols.
- **CCF Members (Civil Contractors Federation)**: Our membership underscores our commitment to industry best practices in civil engineering and construction.
- **Member of the ACA Defence Board**: Highlights our strategic involvement and contribution to defence industry discussions and policy development.
- **ISO accreditation**: Reflects our compliance with international standards in quality, environmental management, and occupational health and safety.
- **Key partnerships**: Collaboration with leading industry and government entities, leveraging global expertise through the Vinci Group.
- **Awards**: Recognition for excellence in construction, safety, and innovation across multiple sectors.
