## Overview

As the authorised supplier of Cat® engines, generators and power solutions in Australia, PNG and the Solomon Islands, we provide Cat products for all power generation requirements across all industries operating from branches around Australia. For Government & Defence, our Marine Excellence Centre (MEC) provides a full-service offering, with technical support throughout system design, equipment specification, installation, commissioning, and class survey process.

We give our customers the sustainable power they need.

## Discriminators

We provide B2B solutions for power and propulsion systems within traditional energy and alternative energy / hybris solutions. We have a strong presence and footprint within the maritime defence industry powering patrol boats, frigates, LHDs, mine hunters, AWDs and Landing Crafts. A Marine Excellence Centre has been strategically headquartered in WA to provide our key primes the support and assistance needed from inception to completion of these large complex projects. We are also very active within the 4 major pillars as industry institutions and organisations namely, Defence West (as part of DJTSI), AIDN, Henderson Alliance and ODIS.
