## Overview

At De Stefano & Co, our mission is to support engineering, manufacturing and technology-based businesses to navigate the complex defence industry environment and grow their defence sector revenues. We do this through a deep understanding of how the defence sector works, its procurement mechanisms, security requirements and the available grant programs.
## Capabilities

De Stefano & Co supports businesses through the following services:

- STRATEGY**

- Defence Industry Market Entry (DIME(r)) Program
- Defence Sector Due Diligence Activity
- Cyber Security Strategy Review & Uplift

- SECURITY**

- Defence Industry Security Program (DISP) Membership Attainment
- DISP Ongoing Compliance Assurance (OCA(tm))
- Security Officer Support as a Service (SOSaaS(tm))
- Cyber Security Consulting & Maturity Uplift
  - CMMC
