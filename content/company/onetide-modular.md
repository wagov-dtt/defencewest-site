## Overview

Onetide was born in Perth in 2015 from Principal Engineer and founder, Roy Mitchell's extensive expertise in the offshore Oil and Gas industry and a desire to share this knowledge and capability across a broader industry base. The overprint of these efficiencies is lower carbon footprint by doing it once and doing it right the first time, which is an integral part of the Onetide philosophy.

With six decades of technical and operational experience in high-risk industry Onetide deftly provides rapid, accurate, cost-effective delivery. Routinely project managing high-risk multi million budget work programmes over short 30-day campaigns to five-six year programmes. Involving project and budget planning, stakeholder liaison, project management and execution with high technical and HSE risk exposure.
## Capabilities

Onetide is a 100% Australian owned and operated Engineering Innovation Company. Designing and delivering novel engineering solutions for efficiency and safety optimization across a broad range of industry sectors. Championing sovereign capability and support of local SMEs, we provide technical limit innovative solutions to Defence, Mining, Oil and Gas, Marine Logistics, Disaster Relief, and Renewable Energy sectors.

Our capabilities include:

- Advisory and Engineering services
- Novel concept design, prototyping, and production
- Efficient, expedient, on time & budget as low as reasonably practicable (ALARP) risk engineering solutions
- 3D concept modelling
- Additive manufacturing
- Remote site operations optimisation

Our core and patented products include:

- Universal Shore Power Cable & Hose Management SystemTM -  expedites shore power cable and fuel hose handling, national award recipient, in association with our partner Ventia.
  - **Australian Defence Contractor Innovation 2020**
  - ****Australian Workplace Health & Safety Award 2021****
- Mission DeckTM -  no-weld seafastening system enabling rapid marine logistics turn around on vessels of opportunity in "one tide".
- Mobile MicrogridTM -  scalable, mobile, grid quality, hybrid renewable power supply and integrated water purification, anywhere with minimal logistics, and negligible HSER footprint. Scalable from two-person portable helicopter deployable units to multi-megawatt utility scale SPSs.
- Thermal CloakTM - Signal protection, IR cloak, EMP/ECM protection, EMI/RFI shielding.

Onetide's point of difference is the ability to consider a multitude of problems and draw upon our technical limit expertise with modern and additive sovereign manufacturing capability to provide innovative, practical and safe solutions.
