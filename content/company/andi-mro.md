## Overview

ANDI MRO is an Australian-owned, Perth based Maintenance, Repair and Overhaul specialist with more than three decades of sovereign capability in aerospace component and on-wing services. Operating as a CASA Part 145 approved maintenance organisation, ANDI delivers fully integrated component maintenance across NDT, wheels and brakes, landing gear systems, machining and surface protection - from a single facility, under a single quality assurance framework. 

Supporting Australia’s defence and aviation operators since the early 1990s - including the RAAF, RSAF, Jet Aviation (Formerly Hawker Pacific) and major civilian primes - ANDI MRO has now expanded its service offering to naval and wider defence requirements.
## Capabilities

- Non-Destructive Testing (NDT / NDI) - PT, MT, ET, UT & RT Level 2 & 3 (EN4179)
- Aviation Component Maintenance, Repair & Overhaul, Wheels, brakes, landing gears, actuators
- Machining & Manufacturing, in house, on demand
- Surface Protection & Electroplating
- Responsive AOG / Critical support, including on-wing NDT 

- Reduced turnaround time through total in house repair workflow
- Minimised AOG risk through proactive rotable stock management
- Single source, single markup, cost competitive to Defence budget
- Fully managed maintenance lifecycle, collection, repair, release and return
