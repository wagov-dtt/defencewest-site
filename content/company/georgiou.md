## Overview

Georgiou Group is an Australian owned, leading building and civil construction company, delivering a wide range of construction services professionally and capably. With more than 45 years' experience and located in Western Australia, New South Wales, Queensland and Northern Territory, it is our vision to be 'the best people to work with'. We do this by working with our clients and partners to achieve the best possible results. Our focus is achieving excellent project outcomes for customers and pursuing innovative solutions, with first-class safety outcomes.
## Capabilities

Georgiou has extensive experience in the planning, mobilisation and logistical management of resources to develop infrastructure in metropolitan and remote areas.

The company's core capabilities are building construction, urban and land development and regeneration, earthworks, road and bridge construction, precast, marine and port works, and water infrastructure. Georgiou have made significant contributions to developing infrastructure in metropolitan and remote locations, and continually strive to stay one step ahead through the application of innovation in every project. Georgiou aims to minimise any impact on the environment and to ensure cost efficiencies by reusing, recycling and replacing whenever possible to deliver best-for-project outcomes.
