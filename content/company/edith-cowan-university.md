## Overview

An accredited Defence Industry Security Program Member, ECU is supporting Defence and defence industry through programs on cyber security, engineering and science, and health sciences.
## Capabilities

**Cyber Security & Information Warfare**

Recognised as one of Australia's first Academic Centres of Cyber Security Excellence by the Australian Government, ECU delivers onsite bespoke cyber security training to Defence personnel, and is conducting Defence research in:

- Defence personnel cyber training
- Digital twins for automation
- Maritime anomaly detection (AI/ML)
- Electronic systems vulnerability testing

**Maritime**

Our maritime capability is centre on advancing technologies through cutting-edge research and innovation to develop reliable systems and ensuring robust and intelligent solutions for the evolving challenges of the marine domain.

- Human-machine communications
- Reliable underwater messaging systems
- Maritime anomaly detection
- Autonomous systems research
- Marine biosecurity

**Engineering**

ECUs engineering expertise focuses on delivering innovative solutions that enhance efficiency, performance and durability. By leveraging cutting-edge manufacturing techniques and advanced structural design, we create resilient and high-performing systems tailored to meet the demands of modern applications.

- Advanced materials manufacturing
- High-performance and lightweight structures
- Material performance and durability

**Health Sciences**

ECUs researchers have strong cross-disciplianary alliances and collaborate extensively with relevant domestic and international organisations to ensure that our research addresses real-world problems and has direct impact at an individual, community and global level.

- Virtual-reality-enhanced tactical parachute training.
- Physical injury prevention
- Digital twinning development
- Virtual Reality Mass Casualty Triage Simulator

**Availability & Support**

Bespoke research and education offerings can be tailored to meet Defence and Defence Industry requirements, with a range of ECU facilities also available for Defence and Defence Industry to conduct research.
