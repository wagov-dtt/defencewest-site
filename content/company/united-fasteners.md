## Overview

Fastener supplier to ASC for the Collins Class for 20+ years, supplier to AWD Hobart class, Offshore Patrol vessels, ANZAC class frigates, Hunter Class Frigates and current AWD upgrade project. 
## Capabilities

Global sourcing capabilities from manufacturers of commercial and custom military spec fasteners. We employ a technical engineer to provide advice on bolted joints and fastener selection/design.  We have been servicing and the Collins class with product and advice for over 20 years.
