## Overview

We service over a hundred clients across the nation and the Pacific, including prime defence contractors such as Austal Ships, Babcock, BAE, Navantia, Norship and Thales. Our commercial works are also extensive with major marine operators including Bhagwan Marine, Birdon Marine, MSC, Sea Swift, Serco and Svitzer both in port and as riding gangs.

Griffin Marine Services prides itself on proactive and professional support service to all clients. Our testimonials are proof we deliver high-quality and trustworthy service in the shortest time.
