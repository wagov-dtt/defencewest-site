## Overview

Established in 2010, EIG Australia is a team of highly experienced professionals in the Electrical & Mechanical Industry. With a strong background in building hydrocarbon infrastructure in the Australian Defence Force, Mining and Commercial Sectors, our directors bring unparalleled industry knowledge to our clients.

At EIG Australia, we take pride in exceeding our clients' expectations by providing tailored total solution packages. Our commitment to quality workmanship ensures that every project is completed to the highest standard, instilling confidence in our clients.

Our comprehensive range of services includes planning, design, construction, and ongoing maintenance. We value close collaboration with our clients, working closely with them at every stage of the project to fully understand and meet their requirements.

When you choose to partner with EIG Australia, you can be confident in connecting with a team of experts dedicated to delivering quality infrastructure solutions.
