## Overview

Grant Transformers was founded in 1931 in Western Australia, Grant Transformers are experts in manufacturing and supplying of Isolation, Power & Distribution transformers as well as Industrial DC Power Supplies and related specialised electrical solutions for mining, military, industrial and commercial sectors. We offer in house design and support capability with local manufacturing and engineering in Perth.
## Capabilities

Grant Transformers have been operating in Australia for 89 years, and have full accreditation in Quality Management Systems including ISO9001, ISO14001, ISO45001. Our company has a long history of delivering high quality and bespoke solutions to customers including Naval applications.
