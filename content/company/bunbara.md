## Overview

Bunbara has been proud to engage within the defence industry, rooted in our commitment to excellence and innovation. As an Aboriginal female-owned business, we have brought a unique perspective and expertise to the sector, delivering high-quality products and services that meet stringent defence standards. Bunbara has established itself as a trusted partner within the industries it works, known for our ability to integrate cultural insights with technical proficiency. Our journey in the defence industry is marked by successful collaborations and a dedication to supporting national security through reliable and culturally informed solutions.

## Capabilities

Bunbara offers a range of capabilities that are highly beneficial to the defence industry, combining technical expertise with cultural insight. Our core competencies include procurement services, supply chain management, and the provision of specialised piping components, plate and valves in materials that meet rigorous defence standards. We pride ourselves on our ability to deliver innovative solutions tailored to the unique needs of defence clients.

Recently, Bunbara successfully collaborated with a leading defence contractor to supply valves and other equipment. Our team leveraged suppliers and our knowledge to meet the specific requirements of this project, demonstrating our capacity for high-quality production and adaptability.

Additionally we can provide procurement services and management of the procurement process for projects/packages. Through our procurement services, Bunbara not only supports operational efficiency but also promotes inclusive practices that reflect the values of our clients in the defence sector.

These examples underscore Bunbara's commitment to providing exceptional service and value to the defence industry through our unique blend of technical capability and cultural understanding.
