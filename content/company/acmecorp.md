## Overview

ACME Corporation is a Western Australian company specialising in innovative defence solutions. Founded in 1949, we have grown to become a trusted supplier of advanced equipment and engineering services to the defence sector.

Our team of engineers and technicians work closely with prime contractors and government agencies to deliver tailored solutions that meet the unique requirements of modern defence operations.

## Capabilities

- Advanced robotics and autonomous systems
- Rapid prototyping and custom fabrication
- Engineering design and consultation
- Equipment maintenance and sustainment
- Testing and evaluation services

## Discriminators

### Australian-Made Innovation
All products are designed and manufactured in Western Australia, ensuring sovereign capability and local supply chain resilience.

### Rapid Response
Our agile team can deliver prototype solutions within weeks, not months, enabling faster capability development cycles.

### Defence Experience
Over 75 years of experience supporting Australian Defence Force operations, with deep understanding of military requirements and procurement processes.
