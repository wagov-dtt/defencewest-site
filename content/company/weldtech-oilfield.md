## Overview

Weldtech oilfield services is a mutli-disciplined maintenance and construction contractor specialising in providng the highest level of fabrication services to the oil & gas, marine and mining industries throughout australia. In the last decade we have earned a reputation for excellent service, unique, individualised and specialised providing flexibility, quality and expertise.

## Capabilities

We have our own work crews and mobile equipment that can be deployed throughout Australia. We possess the capability of taking on complex or urgent construction and maintenance tasks, which often require specialized approaches and techniques.

Our expertise includes:

  * wharf's, jetties and marine structures
  * marine vessel and platform mobilisations structural steel supply and erection
  * piping supply and installation
  * mechanical supply and erection
  * construction and maintenance services

Weldtech Oilfield Services carry out a range of marine vessel and platform mobilizations including project managing or supervising the project.

In the most recent time Weldtech Oilfield Services have engaged in the scope of works:

  * contracted and completed works at the Moresby Wharf Replacement and site welding works at Garden Island HMAS Stirling
  * supplied and Fabricated Parks and Oxley Fender Boards for Garden Island HMAS Stirling
  * supplied and completed scope of works on HMAS Serius fuel piping and replacement of high pressure hydraulic lines
  * supplied an fabricated steel works for Armament Wharf - HMAS Stirling Garden Island
  * registered members of the Henderson Alliance Group within Henderson and the AMC, WA
  * we have the ability to provide the marine and defense sector with a fully integrated service for the repair, maintenance of naval and commercial vessels. We have multi disciplinary capabilities and facilities to conduct scope of works at our facility in Henderson or mobilise at the AMC facility as we are located in very close proximity to AMC and surrounding areas.
  * *our quality systems are qualified to ISO9001:2015
  * we are a DNVGL approved welding workshop facility
