## Overview

Established in 2010, based in the Australian Marine Complex (AMC) in Western Australia, we have engineered and manufactured custom solutions for the evolving needs of Defence, Maritime, Offshore and Industrial industries. Delivering a multi-disciplined in-house team of highly skilled designers, trades and craftspeople, Echo Marine Group deliver a consistently high standard of service, engineering, workmanship and finish across all newbuild, refit, maintenance and repair projects.

## Capabilities

Echo Marine Group is an experienced and respected team supplying high-quality products and services to the Defence Industry. Defence projects benefit with the same care and attention to detail as we offer our Superyacht clients.
