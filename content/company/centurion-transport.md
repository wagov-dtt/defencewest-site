## Overview

Centurion Transport Pty Ltd is primarily known for its logistics services to the mining and energy sectors, its history with the defence industry has grown significantly through strategic acquisitions. A key milestone occurred in 2023 when Centurion provided logistics solutions to the Singapore Government for armed forces training exercises within Australia.

Centurion is a premier Australian heavy haulage and explosives logistics provider with deep integration into Defence-related supply chains. Its credentials include:

-	Explosives & Dangerous Goods: Centurion handles High Explosives (Class 1), including ANFO, detonators, boosters, and ammunition.

-	Heavy Haulage: Centurion maintains Australia's largest specialised fleet, capable of transporting over-dimensional loads exceeding 200 tonnes, essential for moving military assets and infrastructure.

-	National Security Compliance: Operations are backed by ISO 9001:2015 certification and strict adherence to security-sensitive transport regulations

## Capabilities

Core Defence-Aligned Capabilities

Remote Site Logistics: Expertise in operating across Western Australia, the Northern Territory, and Queensland allows them to support remote training areas and offshore naval facilities.

Explosives & Dangerous Goods (Class 1): Centurion is highly credentialed in transporting ammunition, detonators, and high explosives.

Heavy Haulage & Over-Dimensional Transport: Their fleet can move assets exceeding 200 tonnes, making them suitable for transporting armored vehicles, heavy plant equipment, and modular infrastructure.

Decarbonisation & Resilience: Centurion recently launched a fully operational off-grid electric truck fleet (July 2025), demonstrating a commitment to energy-resilient supply chains that can operate independently of traditional fuel grids.

Recent Examples in Defence:

-	Singapore Armed Forces Support: Centurion manages complex logistics for the Singapore Government during large-scale military training exercises in Australia, involving the movement of sensitive military equipment and personnel supplies.
-	Specialised Military Logistics: The company actively recruits MC Drivers for High Explosives and dangerous goods, specifically supporting regional defense corridors in Rockhampton and Brisbane.
-	Infrastructure & Heavy Haulage Projects: In 2025, Centurion continued to leverage its heavy haulage expertise to deliver massive equipment for industrial partners like Liebherr-Australia, utilizing the same specialized trailers required for heavy military vehicle recovery and transport.
