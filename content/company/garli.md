## Overview

Garli is a successful civil construction and contracting business. Our success has been built on our ability to deliver quality projects and with very high Indigenous employment and engagement KPI's. Garli believes that Aboriginal people have a part to play in the Defence Forces across all levels. Garli undertakes the training and sign off to successfully upskill people to a particular skill that the Defence Forces require.
