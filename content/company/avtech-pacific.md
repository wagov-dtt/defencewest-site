## Overview

Founded in 1979 with a focus for taking on machining projects no one else would touch due to the nature of their complexity Avtech has grown significantly. Our loyal customers entrust us with their valued projects across many industries including Defence, Oil and Gas, Mining and Medical. Quality and customer success are what sets us apart from our competition. We deliver on our customers requirements always. With a defect rate of less than 0.1% you can be sure your parts with be in spec, on time.
## Capabilities

Our organisation is unique to the WA Defence industry as our investment in quality, process and controls for customer success is unrivalled. Our projects arrive on time, to spec, complete with compliance document packs. Our team has a defect rate of less than 0.1% and an average delivery time of +1 days (early). This is exceptional for industry and we are always looking to raise the bar on this standard. We are ISO 9001, 45001 accredited and are constantly working to improve our technology, tooling and training. Our investment in machinery gives us the ability to deliver 5 axis turning, 4 axis milling, wire cutting to 400mm and CMM measuring. We also offer 3rd party service provider management which creates logistical efficiencies for our client base.
