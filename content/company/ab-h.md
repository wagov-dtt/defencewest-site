## Overview

AB&H Pty Ltd is a Technology Services company specialising in Agile Program and Project Management. Our project managers are PRINCE2 and PMI certified.\
AB&H Pty Ltd also provides professional services for ICT Technology planning - including:,

- Cyber Security
- Applications Requirements Planning
- Infrastructure Planning
- Web Design

For eligible companies, AB&H Pty Ltd can facilitate R&D Project funding by registering eligible companies with The Department of Industry, Science, Energy & Resources, to enable companies to claim the R&D Tax Incentive, providing (up to) 43.5% of R&D Expenditure that has already been spent in a Financial Year.
## Capabilities
