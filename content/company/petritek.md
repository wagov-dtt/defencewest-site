## Overview

Founded in Perth, Western Australia, Petritek has established itself as a leader in providing R&D-driven solutions for industries operating in challenging environments. Originally focused on the Oil & Gas and Mining sectors, our expertise in precision engineering, advanced monitoring systems, and radiation-based technologies naturally positioned us to explore opportunities within the Defence industry. Over time, we have refined our expertise to address the unique challenges of Defence applications, focusing on delivering precise, dependable, and tailored solutions.

Petritek's core strength lies in developing bespoke solutions tailored to specific operational challenges. With experience delivering advanced transport solutions for the Space sector and precision instrumentation for subsea operations, we bring a multi-disciplinary approach to Defence. Our isotopic-based tracking systems and environmental monitoring technologies, already proven in extreme conditions, offer scalable applications for Defence logistics, surveillance, and infrastructure integrity.

We are committed to collaborating with Defence partners to align our solutions with the unique demands of national security. Whether it's providing cutting-edge detection systems, transport innovations, or maintenance tools for sensitive assets, Petritek seeks to deliver value by enhancing operational efficiency and reducing risks.

Looking ahead, Petritek is actively expanding its Defence capabilities through strategic partnerships, ongoing R&D investments, and a dedicated focus on innovation. By leveraging our existing expertise and applying it to Defence needs, we aim to be a trusted partner in safeguarding Australia's critical infrastructure and advancing technological excellence in this vital sector.
