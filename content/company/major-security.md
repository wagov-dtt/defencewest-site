## Overview

For over 16 years, Major Security has been a trusted provider of security guard services across a diverse spectrum of industries. Our journey has been marked by experience, excellence, and a commitment to safeguarding our clients' interests. Major Security have provided security services for Department of Defence (WA) at:

1. Garden Island Naval Base in Rockingham
2. Irwin Barracks in Karrakatta
3. Leeuwin Barracks in East Fremantle
4. Palmer Barracks in South Guildford
5. Pearce Airforce Base in Bullsbrook
6. Swanbourne SAS Base
7. Major Security also managed Sentry duties at Lancelin and Bindoon firing ranges.
## Capabilities

Major Security, with over 16 years of industry expertise, offers a comprehensive suite of security services. Our range of services consist of:

1. **Security Personnel:** Our trained security guards provide vigilant protection for our clients' premises, events, and assets.
2. **Advanced Surveillance:** We deploy state-of-the-art CCTV cameras to monitor and deter threats effectively.
3. **Eco-Friendly Solutions:** Our solar-powered surveillance systems ensure uninterrupted coverage while minimising environmental impact.
4. **Alarm Expertise:** From installation to ongoing maintenance, we safeguard your space with reliable alarm systems.
5. **Training Excellence:** As a Registered Training Organisation, we empower individuals with nationally recognized security courses.

At Major Security, our vision is to provide superior quality security services to our valued clients. We are fully committed to delivering our services with the utmost professionalism and to the highest possible standards.
