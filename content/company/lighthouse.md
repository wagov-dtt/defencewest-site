## Overview

Lighthouse Engineering Pty Ltd (LHE) has its base in Western Australia and is locally owned and operated since 2012. We have earned our reputation for specialising in general steel fabrications and steel structures. We provide cost effective and reliable structural steel and metal works, compliant with Australian standards.

We have successfully completed projects in the areas of infrastructure including airports, Defence, commercial, industrial, and resources sector.
## Capabilities

Ability to support Defence construction project with the structural steel scope from steel detailing all the way to install.

Experienced team who understands Defence projects uniqueness in terms of quality and security.

We have successfully performed projects on Campbell Barracks and HMAS Stirling:

- Campbell Barracks - Mock Up Range Refurbishment: Steel detailing, supply and install of structural steel.
- HMAS Stirling - HMAS-BU1-E13 CRASH GATE: Design, supply, install PAS68 crash barrier.
- HMAS Stirling - NCIS3_N2262_STAGE 2 - Support User Facility: Steel detailing, supply and install of structural steel, including install of precast panels.
