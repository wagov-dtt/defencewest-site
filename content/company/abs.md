## Overview

ABS Welding has been in business for over 19 years and specializes in the Supply & Install of structural steel. We have worked on defence projects on Garden Island and the swan barracks delivering onsite welding services and structural steel components. We specialize in the residential, commercial and industrial sectors all over Western Australia.
## Capabilities

ABS supply and install steelwork to the Commercial, Industrial and Residential sectors. Some of our clients include UGL, Kaefer, Mapel, BBD and DMG. Our supply capabilities range from 1-1000 Tonne projects. We have recently finished the supply and delivery of structural steel to the Alcoa upgrades on all three refineries on behalf of UGL.

We have a large onsite service which provides maintenance and support to all clients. Our extensive in-house capabilities can provide the total package; procurement, fabrication, treatment through to installation. ABS has a Quality Management System in place which is certified to ISO 9001-2015 Standard
