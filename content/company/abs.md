## Overview

ABS has evolved from a Western Australian welding and fabrication service into a company increasingly aligned with the needs of Australia’s Defence industry. Our history is grounded in delivering high‑quality, standards‑driven steel fabrication, mobile welding, and inspection services across industrial and infrastructure projects. Over time, our strong QA culture, certified welding procedures, and commitment to controlled processes positioned us naturally toward Defence‑relevant work. In recent years, ABS has actively pursued Defence and nuclear‑adjacent capability uplift, strengthening documentation control, material traceability, and workforce qualifications to meet the expectations of modern Defence supply chains. This progression reflects our long‑term commitment to supporting Australia’s strategic industrial base.
## Capabilities

ABS provides a focused set of capabilities that directly strengthen Defence supply chains, combining high‑integrity welding, disciplined quality systems, and mobile industrial support. Our team delivers certified structural and pressure‑related fabrication, supported by strong QA, material traceability, and controlled welding procedures suitable for Defence‑grade work. We offer rapid‑deployment mobile welding and site services across Western Australia, enabling maintenance, repair, and infrastructure support for bases, shipyards, and industrial facilities. With growing alignment to Defence and nuclear‑adjacent requirements, investment in workforce upskilling, and a culture built on reliability and compliance, ABS brings practical, high‑standards‑driven capability to Defence projects and prime contractors.
## Discriminators

ABS stands out through its disciplined approach to quality, its deep technical welding capability, and its ability to deliver reliable outcomes in demanding industrial environments. Unlike many general fabrication providers, ABS combines certified high‑integrity welding with rigorous material traceability, controlled documentation, and inspection processes aligned with Defence expectations. Our mobile welding units and field‑ready workforce provide rapid, flexible support across Western Australia, while our strong QA culture ensures repeatability, compliance, and confidence for clients operating in regulated sectors. This blend of technical skill, operational responsiveness, and standards‑driven execution distinguishes ABS as a dependable partner for Defence and other high‑assurance industries.
