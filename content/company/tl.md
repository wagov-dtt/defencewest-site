## Overview

***TL Engineering*** **started in 1970 as a small backstreet business in Perth WA.**

Today TL Engineering is a leading motor vehicle body builder based in Western Australia, specialising in the design, manufacture, and supply of high-quality, custom-built commercial and retail vehicle bodies for a variety of industries.

Established in 1970, TL Engineering has been at the forefront of the industry, offering innovative solutions to meet the unique requirements of our clients. With over 50 years of experience, we have earned a reputation for excellence and reliability, providing our customers with durable and efficient vehicle bodies that exceed expectations.

Our team of highly skilled fabricators and technicians work closely with clients to understand their specific needs and design custom solutions that meet their exact requirements. We use only the highest quality materials and modern manufacturing techniques to ensure that our products are built to last.

At TL Engineering, we take pride in our commitment to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction. All our vehicle bodies undergo rigorous testing and quality control measures to ensure that they meet the highest industry standards.

Our product range includes a wide variety of custom-built vehicle bodies, including but not limited to service bodies, truck bodies, Ute trays, alloy trays and canopies, and more. Our focus on innovation and customisation allows us to offer a wide range of options and configurations to suit any application.

***We are proud to be an industry leader in Western Australia, and we are looking to partner with Defence for future opportunities for general fabrication. We have a large talented team of fabricators and capacity to service Defence.***
