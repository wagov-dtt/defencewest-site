## Overview

Wedgetail Aerospace is a Western Australian Drone Operations and Training Company founded by a Western Australian entrepreneur and veteran owned company that had seen the utility of large and long-range drones in operational theatres. Our founders sought to realise the capability to operate this step change technology in commercial airspace and provide aerial intelligence, surveillance, reconnaissance, data acquisition and time sensitive air freight to Defence, Government Agencies and commercial customers. Wedgetail Aerospace's approvals to operate in commercial airspace enables unencumbered utility for Defence and Government agencies enabling contracting and application of this unique capability on a task-based approach.
## Capabilities

- Long-range and complex Beyond Visual Line of Sight (BVLOS) drone operations to conduct intelligence, surveillance, reconnaissance, survey and/or air freight over the land and maritime environment
- Test and evaluation of drones to determine what platform and systems can best service customer requirements
- Integration, test and evaluation of sensors and payloads to determine what systems can best service customer requirements
- Drone training to achieve Civil Aviation Safety Authority (CASA) Licenses and enable customers to conduct real world operations
## Discriminators

- Best in class pilots, maintainers, communications architects and engineers with thousands of hours of crewed and uncrewed aviation experience
- A ready to go mobile operations centre that enables rapid deployment to conduct operations in remote areas
- Access to expansive training and development areas in uncontrolled airspace that enables training of pilots, test and evaluation of drones, sensors and payloads and development of complex and long-range mission profiles
- The skill and experience required to realise the regulatory approvals required to conduct complex and long-range drone operations
- The only approval in Australia to operate drones >150kg takeoff weight
- The ability to source financing and insurance for customers drones, sensors and payloads
- Approvals for from the Civil Aviation Safety Authority (CASA) to conduct drone operations for multi-rotor, fixed wing, powered lift and helicopter drones, by day, night and beyond visual line of sight
- Civil Aviation Safety Authority (CASA) approved Training Organisation that can issue licenses for drone pilots
