## Overview

Established in 2001, Calytrix Technologies is an Australian owned and operated Training Systems Integrator (TSI) with offices in Perth, Canberra, Orlando Florida and Bristol in the UK. Calytrix specialises in the integration of Training, Simulation and Engineering capabilities to deliver world-leading Live, Virtual and Constructive (LVC) training solutions. Providing highly qualified training experts supported by a team of talented software and simulation engineers, Calytrix blends experience and technology to deliver world-class training environments. From complex joint and collation exercise design and management to the creation of innovative simulators, Calytrix is a proven TSI with the experience and people to support your training, simulation and engineering needs. Today Calytrix's customers include the ADF (all services), Austal, Boeing, Collins Aerospace, Lockheed Martin, Raytheon etc.as well as export customers across North America and Europe.
## Capabilities

Calytrix specialises in the integration of training systems and engineering capabilities to deliver world-leading defence training solutions. Within our simulation technology expertise, Calytrix provides systems engineering and architectural design, simulation software, advanced simulated radio communications and advanced 3D virtual reality development environments.

Calytrix's products and services include:

- Training needs analysis and design (SADL)
- Simulation network and architecture design and delivery
- Simulation software
  - Whole of world 3D VR synthetic environments
  - Advanced simulated radio communications
- Emulated military equipment
- Virtual and Augmented Reality
- Exercise design and delivery

With 75+ full-time staff, Calytrix maintains ISO 9001 Quality, 27001 Information Security and 45001 Safety certification, holds DISP membership.
## Discriminators

With more than 20-years' experience delivering complex high-tempo training and simulation systems to the ADF, and a unique understanding of joint and collective exercise domain, Calytrix has a deep and proven understanding of defence training. Uniquely we are able to support our customers from their initial concept and design phases; training and systems development; and into service and sustainment. We deliver a full-spectrum training offering to support the warfighter.
