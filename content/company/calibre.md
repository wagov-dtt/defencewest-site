## Overview

Calibre Group comprises a diverse group of capabilities including: engineering design services; consulting services; technology, communications and control systems; urban development; project management and EPCM; maintenance and shutdown services. These capabilities are delivered across several sectors including Defence, resources, government, commercial and private.
## Capabilities

The diverse nature of Calibre's capabilities enables us to apply solutions traditionally employed in one sector, to also be transferred to another sector. This provides customers with value for money implementation of tried and tested solutions.
