## Overview

RCT has the expertise and technologies that transform the way the mining and industrial sectors operate. Specialising in the areas of machine Automation & Control, Protection, and Information systems, RCT is the only company in the world to offer an array of proven solutions that are agnostic and fully interoperable. We help customers to improve the utilisation and performance of their mobile equipment with our vast range of Smart Technology solutions which are backed by exceptional global service, parts, and support network. With 48 years of experience in more than 70 countries worldwide, we provide customers with the advantage of measurable value: increased profitability, productivity, efficiency, and safety.
## Capabilities

RCT is the only company in the world that specialises in all three areas of: Automation & Control, Protection Systems, Information Systems, and Communication Networks. RCT offer an array of proven solutions that are agnostic and fully interoperable. RCT's solutions can be installed on any make or model of mobile machine, including Caterpillar, Komatsu, Sandvik, and Atlas Copco. To date, there have been 3500+ Control solutions installed on 200+ different machine models worldwide.
