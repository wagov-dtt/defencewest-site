## Overview

Everett Smith has been involved with providing Electrical Contracting and Engineering services to the defence industry since June 2021. Works completed at HMAS Stirling and Henderson were recieved and appreciated as of a high standard and quality. Everett Smith continues to provide services to the defence industry, through 2025 and into the future.
## Capabilities

**Defence Sector Experience, Security Clearances & IT Security**

We have extensive experience delivering complex electrical and communications solutions for defence facilities, and our team includes personnel with current NV1 security clearances. In addition, Everett Smith & Co employs advanced IT security measures to ensure all client and project data is protected to the highest standards, meeting the stringent requirements of the defence sector and other sensitive industries.

**Core Competencies:**

- Electrical engineering and design & construct
- High voltage and infrastructure solutions
- Structured cabling, network deployment, and fibre optic systems
- Lighting control implementation, LED upgrades, and thermographic imaging
- Quality assurance and certified management systems (ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001)
- Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) leadership
