## Overview

DISP readiness, triple-ISO certification, and familiarity with Defence security protocols allow us to mobilise
without delay, work in restricted zones, and integrate lifts into sensitive build schedules. By consolidating lifts and
minimising repeat mobilisations, we reduce clearance costs and keep critical-path activities moving.
## Capabilities

Our expertise in custom lifting and rigging: 
Rigsafe provides a complete lifting, rigging, and transport service from early stage lift planning through to project execution. These services are integrated to maximise value for our clients through: 
Project Value Engineering & Planning: 
We engage early to reshape build sequencing, access design, and lift strategies. This shortens programs, lowers costs, and minimises risk on critical-path activities. On smaller projects, site-ready lift plans prevent delays and ensure compliance from day one. 
Installation: 
We manage structural steel erection, precast concrete, timber and façade installations. By sequencing works carefully, we minimise rework, site congestion, and delays. Supported by planning, coordination, and in-house fabrication, our teams deliver safer and more predictable outcomes. Structural steel, precast and facades sequenced to minimise rework and congestion. 
Mechanical Rigging & Skating:
 From chillers and escalators to large-scale machinery, we provide precision installation in tight or sensitive environments. Using advanced methods, we minimise disruption and protect surrounding assets.
