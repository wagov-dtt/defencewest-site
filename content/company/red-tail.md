## Overview

Red Tail Group (RTG) is a local company based in Perth Western Australia, the directors commenced together 2 years ago in 2022 with a shared vision of bringing a boutique and quality service to all industries. We have extensive trade and Mechanical Engineering backgrounds and use this to ensure our clients receive only quality service and advice.

Our aim is not only to provide world-class corrosion prevention and control but an engineered solution to ensure all our client's equipment and materials, are maintained and preserved to ensure costs and downtime are reduced and minimised.
## Capabilities

Red Tail Group (RTG) offers a range of Products, Services & solutions for the Marine, Offshore, Industrial, Mining & Energy Sectors. RTG is the Zerust distributor of Volatile Corrosion Inhibitors (VCI's) in Australia. Zerust has been established globally for over 60 years.

RTG offers the following services:

- Preservation & Maintenance engineering, planning and execution
- Mothballing & Extended Outages
- SCI & VCI Products & Services (Zerust)
- CMMS & Inventory Control
- Project Management Preservation & Maintenance Mothballing
- VCI and Non-VCI Industrial wrapping services (for all equipment)
## Discriminators

RTG and Zerust distinguishes itself from competitors by offering cost-effective and comprehensive Integrated Corrosion Technologies (ICT) that go beyond simply selling products, providing end-to-end Asset Integrity Solutions. This includes a wide range of solutions, expert technical support, global testing, and a focus on customer needs. Zerust's commitment to quality, safety, and the environment is also a key differentiator.

Here's a more detailed breakdown:

- **Holistic Approach:**

Zerust moves beyond product sales to offer complete asset integrity programs, helping clients implement proactive, long-term corrosion mitigation strategies.

- **Integrated Corrosion Technologies (ICT):**

This system combines proprietary inhibitors with traditional methods like cathodic protection and coatings, offering unique and innovative solutions.

- **VCI Expertise:**

Zerust is a leading supplier of Vapor Corrosion Inhibitor (VCI) based solutions, particularly for industries like oil and gas.

- **Customer Service and Support:**

Zerust emphasizes expert technical support and tailored solutions, understanding the specific needs of each client's assets.

- **Global Presence and Reach:**

Zerust has a global presence, with representatives in over 70 countries, ensuring access to expert support and solutions worldwide.

- **Broad Range of Products:**

In addition to VCI, Zerust offers rust preventives, VCI oils, corrosion inhibiting additives, and rust removers.

- **Focus on Prevention and Mitigation:**

RTG & Zerust focuses on preventing corrosion-related failures and reducing associated costs.
