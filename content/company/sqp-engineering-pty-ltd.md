## Overview

Founded in 2006, SQP Engineering is a family-owned WA business built on precision, innovation, and local talent. Over nearly two decades, we’ve expanded from traditional machining to automation, additive manufacturing, and advanced metrology — while keeping our core principles of Service, Quality, and Products at the heart of everything we do.
## Capabilities

SQP Engineering provides advanced, sovereign manufacturing capabilities that directly support Australia’s defence industrial base. As a Western Australian, family‑owned manufacturer, SQP delivers high‑precision, high‑volume production using state‑of‑the‑art CNC machining, additive manufacturing, robotic automation, and digital production systems. These capabilities enable defence and defence primes to access reliable, secure, Australian‑made components and assemblies. 

Our integrated manufacturing environment combines CNC machining, 3D printing, metrology, and automated workflows to deliver consistent quality, tight tolerances, and rapid turnaround for mission‑critical parts. SQP’s cyber‑secure IT systems and digital production planning ensure traceability, repeatability, and compliance with defence‑grade requirements. 

Core Capabilities
High‑precision CNC machining for complex metal and polymer components.
Additive manufacturing (3D printing) for rapid prototyping and production.
Robotic automation to support high‑volume, consistent output.
ISO 9001 supported by advanced automated metrology 
Secure digital manufacturing systems ensuring data integrity and IP protection.
Experience across defence aerospace, marine, oil & gas, and advanced industrial sectors.
