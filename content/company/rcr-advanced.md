## Overview

RCR Advanced Technologies is an award-winning, innovative engineering company with expertise in delivering solutions for the Defence, rail and infrastructure/renewables sectors.

RCR Advanced Technologies is part of RCR Mining Technologies, a market leader with a proud history dating back to 1975 and a strong reputation for delivering high-quality technologies for national and international clients.

Our Marine Safe Access Solution (MSAS) is custom engineered to provide safe and reliable docking and deployment every time and it is designed to work in extreme conditions, saving time and increasing safety and efficiency.

We offer a range of innovative rail transport and unloading technology for bulk commodities and intermodal logistics solutions. RCR has an exclusive licensing agreement with Kiruna Wagon for the manufacture and delivery of a wide range of rail wagon technologies.

Our range of engineering solutions and expertise is tailored to meet the unique requirements of each project. With a focus on innovation and the application of leading-edge, sustainable technologies, our solutions aim to provide improvements in safety, reliability, efficiency and overall value for our clients.
