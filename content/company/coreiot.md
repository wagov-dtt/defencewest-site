## Discriminators

There is a growing demand for digitisation and automation by Manufacturing businesses.

This is where Smart Shop Floor (SSF) was born. SSF was developed in Western Australia in collaboration with leading Western Australian Manufacturers, Lean Experts, and Advanced Manufacturing Growth Centre.

SSF is a digital tool designed to assist SME manufacturers in addressing 3 key fundamental issues:

- Connected Supply Chain - Connecting Defence contractor and Primes with SME businesses to gain traceability, visibility and progress on the work

- Increasing productivity with various lean and Industry 4.0 principles

- Increases throughput and increases margin by optimising the available skills set in business with the use of Automated Scheduling.

  The SME job shops and manufacturers represents over 300,000+ Manufacturing businesses in the AUKUS region.
