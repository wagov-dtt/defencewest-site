## Overview

EK Contracting is a majority Indigenous owned company with a strong focus on Indigenous employment and ongoing career development through our award winning training division. We cater for all your civil and transport needs and can mobilise heavy earth moving equipment, skilled operators and transportation anywhere in WA at short notice. Our scheduler ensures clients are communicated with regularly to ensure project stays within scope and issues are dealt with in a timely manner. Our award winning electronic safety management system monitors all safety aspects via QR codes allowing us to solve problems in real time and communicate these solutions with clients immediately. We are proud to belong and can be contacted on the following details to further discuss our capabilities in greater detail.
## Capabilities

Our purpose is to problem solve and make it easy with new thinking in diversified services. We supply earthmoving, transport and other mobile equipment solutions. Our key customers are FMG, City of Rockingham and Local Shires. EK has a mobile mechanical division comprising heavy diesel mechanics, boiler makers and tyre fitters ready to service our own fleet and others. A compliance culture is embedded at EKC to ensure Safety, Sustainability and Success. EK has just been awarded an ISO grant and will be obtaining ISO 9001, 14001 and 45001 in the next six months. We are currently engaged with ODIS and the Entrepreneurs' Programme through AusIndustry.
