## Overview

OFI specialises in electrical engineering, automation, instrumentation and design and construction. We are at the forefront of electrical innovation and integration as a result of our commitment to active research, development of new ideas and embracing industry evolutions. We are committed to supporting our clients with exceptional electrical solutions that improve their systems and maximise efficiencies.

OFI is a specialist electrical contractor with over 15 years of Defence experience across Australia. Completing works for projects under a variety of challenging conditions, remote areas, OFI prides itself in its ongoing relationship with Department of Defence and has extensive experience in Defence base electrical installations and upgrades. Having previous collaborations with Head Contractor and Managing Contractor models, OFI values safe, secure, efficient and effective design, manufacturing, installation and commissioning processes for long-lasting, quality results. With works completed on Fuel Remediation, Jet Fuel and Space Telescope sites OFI has obtained varied experience and understanding of completing works within the Defence sector.
