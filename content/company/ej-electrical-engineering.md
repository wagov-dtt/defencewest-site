## Overview

EJ Electrical has been providing electrical design, manufacturing, repair, refit and installation services to the marine industry for over 50 years. EJ Electrical Engineering Contractors were established in 1968 as an electrical and electronic engineering company and specialise in the commercial and marine defence fields. Located in the Australian Marine Complex, Henderson, Western Australia which is considered the hub of the Defence, Marine and Oil & Gas Industries.
## Capabilities

With years of experience we have the knowledge to execute a variety electrical tasks. Our Defence team currently service ANZAC warships, submarines and patrol Vessels throughout Australia. We also carry out a range of electrical services to the infrastructure on Garden Island. Our employees are qualified and have a high regard for safety. EJ Electricals aim is to complete all projects on time and to a very high standard.

- Project management
- Design Services
- Experienced Marine Electricians
- Distributor of Marine Parts and cables
- Manufacturing and Switchboards.
- Generators, Motors, Transformers and Controls
- PLC's, VSD's, Instrumentation, Hazardous Area Inspections.
- Mill-spec cabling, Communications and Electronics.
- Compliance Reports
- ISO 90001, ISO 14001, and AS 4801 Accredited
