## Overview

Oropesa Port Management provides specialist services in the maritime and defence sectors. With experience from waterfront, on-deck and underway operations to executive management and strategic capability development and delivery, our team is able to provide pragmatic understanding to complex challenges facing maritime and port entities. Having delivered solutions to tourism, mining, oil and gas, Defence and Governments, local, state and federal, Oropesa has worked across a broad range of sectors that rely on safe and efficient outcomes in ports and on the water. Our people have operated globally in the service of our nation and in the pursuit of commercial success, delivering equal commitment to the achievement of outcomes regardless of the challenge.

## Capabilities

Oropesa holds a unique position in understanding the needs of commercial ambitions and the demands of Defence. Our background and success in both provides insight and understanding not available in other organisations.
