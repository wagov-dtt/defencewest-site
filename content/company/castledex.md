## Overview

Castledex was founded in 1968 as a workplace storage and filing systems business. Many of the innovative time- and space-saving solutions common in offices and workspaces across Australia were introduced by Castledex.

## Capabilities

Through our eye for detail and expert staff. CDX Single Source can provide unparalleled service in all aspects of FF&E procurement, quality furniture and innovative storage solutions.
